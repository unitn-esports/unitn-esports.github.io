Who we are

UniTn E-sports is a student sports association founded in 2022 with the aim of creating a community of students who can share their passion for competitive video games.
The association aims to create student teams that participate in national and international tournaments and competitions, representing the University of Trento in the field of E-sports.
UniTn E-sports organizes both online and offline events and competitions, involving students from different faculties and academic years, and aims to develop an external network of relationships with other universities. In addition, the association also organizes training events for students interested in entering the world of E-sports.
